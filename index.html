Abibat Mogaji For BAT

Welcome to the support website of Late Abibat Mogaji Beneficiaries Vanguard for Bola Ahmed Tinubu (AMBV4BAT).

Our Mission

To solicit supports from all Nigerians and to mobilize strategically across the country from grassroots level in all states towards ensuring transformation of this nation into a robust decentralized democracy with a diverse industrial base, to provide sufficient jobs to a growing urban population; and a sufficient agricultural base, to achieve food security and provide a decent livelihood to the rural population. We seek a nation founded on justice, peace, prosperity for all, regardless of religion, region, ethnicity and class. We seek a Nigeria that is for all Nigerians.

Our Vision

To see Nigeria that works for everyone irrespective of race, beliefs, religion and region towards making sure that Nigeria attain the greatest height Nigerians desired.

Together we can make Nigeria better.
